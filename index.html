Badminton: the only sport where smashing is encouraged 😄🏸

From backyard fun to Olympic dreams, Vector Badminton Club (VBC) in Coquitlam brings the energy. Founded in 2008 by coach Syd Sydorak (40+ years of experience), our volunteer coaches help kids build skills, confidence, and a love for the game in a fun, supportive environment.

Ages 7–18—come play, improve, and maybe even out-smash your parents!

Join a Session

Our Vision

We believe badminton can shape strong, confident young people. At Vector Badminton Club, every athlete is coached with care, challenged at the right pace, and celebrated for effort as much as results.

Our goal is simple: create a positive sports community where players learn discipline, teamwork, and joy in movement—on and off the court.

Our History

Vector Badminton Club began in 2008 with coach Syd Sydorak's mission to provide quality badminton training for youth in Coquitlam. With more than 40 years of coaching experience, Syd built the club on volunteer spirit, inclusive coaching, and a passion for helping kids grow through sport.

Today, VBC continues that tradition by welcoming ages 7 to 18 into a high-energy, supportive environment.
